Welcome to SnailMail!

SnailMail is a new kind of messaging service designed for meaningful, modern communication.

To send a new SnailMail, simply press the center "Compose" button and begin creating the message. SnailMail can be be sent to existing users of the app, or to a new person by entering their email address on the first screen. Messages are delivered 2 to 3 days after they are sent.

Enjoy!
